Delicious Green Juice

Description

An easy, delicious green juice. You can substitute kale for spinach.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • Â¾ cup fresh spinach, or to taste

Steps

  1. Blend coconut milk, banana, mango, and spinach together in a blender until smooth.
  2. Refrigerate until chilled.
